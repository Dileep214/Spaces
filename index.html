About SPACES

Building tomorrow's leaders since 1993

Our Legacy

Established in 1993 by Sri Prakash Educational Society, SPACES Degree College aims to foster higher education in rural areas with traditional and advanced bachelor's courses in Computer Science, Electronics, Biotechnology, and more.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in their chosen fields. Our four core "L" pillars guide our approach: Learned Faculty, Library, Laboratories, and Healthy Learning Environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education, recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and commitment to student success. We strive to create a diverse and inclusive community that prepares students for global challenges.

World-Class Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure for optimal learning

Modern Library

3rd floor library with 1,036 sq ft stack area and 1,296 sq ft reading area seating 90 students. Houses 14,556 books including 3,542 reference books, 15 print journals, 9 newspapers, e-library, and N-List access.

Advanced Laboratories

Science labs, departmental computer labs, Wi-Fi connectivity, smart boards, English lab, e-learning tools including Google Classroom, and specialized equipment for practical learning.

Sports & Recreation

Comprehensive sports facilities including sports fields, gymnasium, and recreational areas. Located along National Highway 16 at Payakaraopeta with 5-acre campus.

Health & Wellness

Ayurvedic hospital, RO water facility, first aid services, herbal garden, and comprehensive health support for students and staff.

Auditorium & Cultural

Modern auditorium for events, cultural activities, and academic functions. Hosts the annual Spandana Youth Festival and various cultural programs.

Campus Amenities

Canteen, guesthouse, transport facilities, parking area, and other essential amenities for a comfortable campus life.

Our Placement Process

1

Pre-placement Training

Comprehensive training conducted by our Training & Placement Cell to prepare students for interviews and technical assessments.

2

Company Visits

Companies conduct interviews and tests either on-campus or virtually, providing direct interaction opportunities.

3

Selection Rounds

Multi-stage selection including aptitude tests, technical interviews, and HR rounds to assess candidate suitability.

4

Offers & Internships

Successful candidates receive job offers or internships, with many internships converting to full-time positions.

Career Development Support

Higher Education Preparation

Support for PG entrance exams like CUET, JAM, or MBA to pursue research or management roles.
